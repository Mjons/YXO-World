The Day's Journey

Your Day of Discovery

Six hours of hands-on learning, collaboration, and discovery

9:00 AM

🎯 Welcome & Icebreaker: Your Extraordinary Future

Build rapport, set expectations, create a safe learning environment. Overview of the day: "Today we're going to think like the people building the future"

9:30 AM

🌍 What's Happening in the World

The World is Changing Faster Than Ever. Explore the Exponential Age (Moore's Law, Law of Accelerating Returns), the 7 Mindsets of an Extraordinary Life.

⚡ 15-Minute Stretch Break — Healthy Snacks & Movement
10:30 AM

🤖 AI Is a Superpower You Can Use

Learn what AI can do today with live examples. Discover how workflows change with AI for school, projects, and careers. Master prompts that 10x your creativity & productivity.

11:15 AM

🚀 Entrepreneurship: Starting Your Own Company

Learn the differences between Entrepreneur vs Employee mindsets. Understand what a company actually is and why teens today can start real businesses for almost $0.

🍽️ 12:00 PM — Lunch Break
1:00 PM

💡 Design Your Extraordinary Life Roadmap

Explore your personal strengths and sense of purpose. Discover your MTP (Massive Transformative Purpose).

1:45 PM

🔍 Understanding the World & Spotting Real Problems

Extraordinary people solve meaningful problems. Learn how to spot problems in your city, community, and school. Develop a Problem Solving Mindset.

2:00 PM

📱 The Device as Your Tool

Learn to use technology as a powerful tool for creation and problem-solving.

2:15 PM

🤝 Hands-on Teambuilding Activity

A fun, hands-on team challenge that teaches design thinking, collaboration, and iteration!

2:45 PM

🎓 Closing Ceremony and Certificates

Celebrate your extraordinary journey and receive your certificate of completion!
